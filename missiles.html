Indian Missile Programs

Although ISRO is primarily a civilian space agency, missile technology overlaps with space vehicle development. Below are key missile systems developed by India (by DRDO):

  • Agni Series – Long-range ballistic missiles
  • Prithvi – Tactical surface-to-surface missiles
  • BrahMos – Supersonic cruise missile (joint venture)
